From Our Kitchen to Your Table

What started as baking for family and friends quickly turned into something more. Our passion for traditional sourdough and simple, wholesome baking inspired us to start a small micro-bakery.

Every loaf is crafted with patience and care, bringing the flavor and quality of real bread back to the community.

Core Values

I fell in love with sourdough and its entire process, not to mention all the added benefits that align with my core values of genuinely wanting to educate and provide people with a cleaner, higher-quality bread product for all its health benefits: digestion, glucose homeostasis, probiotics, better nutrients, and reduced gluten content.
