FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order?

The minimum standard order is 12 cake pops.

Can I mix flavors and finishes in the same order?

Yes. You can build multiple variety groups in one order and mix flavors, coatings, colors, and finishes.

Do I have to request a quote for every custom order?

No. Simpler custom orders can follow the builder and deposit flow. More complex orders may be flagged for review.

How do deposits work?

A 50% deposit reserves your date. Remaining balance is due before pickup or delivery unless otherwise arranged.

What if I need something not shown in the builder?

Add a note in the builder. If anything needs clarification, we can follow up after your order is submitted.

How far ahead should I order?

Earlier is always better, especially for events, custom themes, and larger quantities.

Ready to Start Your Cake Pop Order?

Build your order, review your total, and reserve your date with a deposit. For highly detailed event styling or larger orders, you can still submit for review.
