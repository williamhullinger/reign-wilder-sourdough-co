Our Process

Built on Tradition. Made by Hand.

At Reign & Wilder Sourdough Co, quality starts with the ingredients. We use high-quality flour from trusted sources, including select imported flour chosen specifically for better flavor, texture, and authenticity.

Every product is made using traditional baking methods — mixed, shaped, and handled entirely by hand. We don’t rely on commercial mixers or mass production, which allows us to control every step and deliver a more consistent, better-tasting result.

Everything is made in small batches with simple ingredients and careful attention to detail. While a modern oven ensures consistency and safety, the heart of the process remains hands-on craftsmanship from start to finish.
